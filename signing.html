THE SIGNING OF THE SEASON IN 2017/18








MOHAMED SALAH

Salah has been a huge signing for Liverpool FC and many people and professionals alike have described Salah as the signing of the season with his incredible goal scoring record and phenomenal pace.



Mohamed Salah joined Liverpool FC at the beginning of the 2017/18 season for a fee of 34m from Roma just shy of the club record at the time of 35m for Andy Carroll in 2011.

STATISTICS

6 appearances in the Champions League with 5 goals and 1 assist

18 appearances in the Premier League with 15 goals and 5 assists.

An average rating of 7.82.

Salah also secured a place in the World Cup for Egypt by scoring a last minute winner via penalty kick and was seen as a hero.

Liverpool FC currently sit 4th in the league and are very happy with their new signing leaving fans at home excited to see more from such a dangerous player in and around the box.
