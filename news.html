ARSENAL: ALEXIS SANCHEZ

Sanchez has been in the centre of transfer sagas with nobody knowing what will be the next step for him with rumours that Barcelona, Man City and Man United interested in signing the 29 year old from Chile.

No doubt Sanchez is aware of these rumours as he hasn't been at the form that he's capable of, in 16 games he's only scored five goals and three assists compared to last season when he had 12 goals and five assists at the same stage.
